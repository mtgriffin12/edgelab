# Strategy Research Principles

- Every strategy must be testable.
- Every strategy must have explicit universe, signal, entry, exit, sizing, risk, and holding-period rules.
- Strategies are rejected by default until evidence supports them.
- Robustness matters more than peak backtest return.
- Strategies must be compared against benchmark behavior.
- Strategies must be tested across market regimes.
- Strategy ideas must include failure conditions.
- No strategy may become paper-trading eligible without evidence.
