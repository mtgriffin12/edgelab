# Strategy Ranking Engine

## Purpose

The Strategy Ranking Engine helps EdgeLab decide which strategies and discovery ideas deserve
deeper testing. It ranks research evidence, not actions.

## Ranking Is Not A Recommendation

Rankings do not decide what to use with real money. They do not create orders, connect to brokers,
or promote ideas to paper or live execution. Real-money use remains blocked.

## Why Total Return Alone Is Dangerous

A high sample return can hide fragile behavior. A strategy with a large worst drop, too few
examples, unsupported logic, or weak data quality should rank lower even if the headline return
looks interesting.

## What Matters

The ranking engine should consider:

- Worst drop control.
- Consistency.
- Sample size.
- Baseline comparison.
- Cost fragility.
- Data quality.
- Curve-fit risk.
- Current environment fit, when available.

## Known And Novel Ideas

Known strategies and novel ideas can both rank well when evidence supports them. Novelty is not
rewarded by itself. A novel idea must improve evidence versus a simpler comparison.

## Research Triage

Ranking is a triage tool. Its job is to say:

- Which ideas deserve deeper testing.
- Which ideas are interesting but fragile.
- Which ideas have insufficient evidence.
- Which ideas should be rejected for now.

Every ranking should explain the bottom line, what helped, what hurt, what evidence is missing,
and why real-money use is not allowed.
