# Operating Model

EdgeLab follows a research lifecycle:

1. Generate a strategy idea.
2. Convert it to a structured strategy specification.
3. Backtest it.
4. Stress test it.
5. Rank it against alternatives.
6. Paper trade it.
7. Monitor live paper behavior.
8. Compare paper results to backtest expectations.
9. Promote, revise, or reject it.

Live trading is future-only and gated. No strategy should move toward real-money execution without explicit future authorization, deterministic risk controls, and a validated promotion path.
