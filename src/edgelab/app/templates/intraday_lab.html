Intraday Lab

First-hour setup research using measurable synthetic events instead of chart intuition.

Current conclusion: this is {{ plain_label("synthetic_intraday_data")|lower }} only. It is not live, not a signal system, not a recommendation, and real-money status is Not allowed.

What this spike studies

Initial synthetic examples use S&P 500-style and Nasdaq-style fixtures, but the system is designed to support other symbols once suitable intraday data and instrument assumptions are available. The goal is to test whether opening levels, candle behavior, failures, momentum, and sit-out sessions can be explained as data.

Setup families

  • Opening range breakout
  • Opening range failure
  • Failed opening push
  • Failed opening selloff
  • Gap fade study
  • Sit-out day

No-setup philosophy

Doing nothing is valid when the synthetic first hour is choppy, too narrow, or conflicting.
